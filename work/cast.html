The Studio

Cast's workflow begins with a recording, and we made it really easy to connect with friends and get right into it.

The Editor

With Cast, we set out to provide a simple and functional alternative to a typical podcasting workflow. We ended up building a multi-track editor right in the browser.

The Publisher

Once the audio is ready, Cast makes it easy to send it out to the world, with hosting and permalink management, along with analytics.
