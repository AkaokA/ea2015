Blaseball Web Redesign

Blaseball was unusual in the video game landscape as its primarily text-based sports-betting-adjacent format lent itself to a more app-like presentation than most indie games on the market.

Upon joining the team, I began overhauling the visual style and interaction design of the product, applying much of its quirky, dark vibe to a comprehensive and modernized visual style—along with a screen-by-screen redesign of the game: from information architecture and navigation, to accessible colours, iconography, and text styles.

Blaseball Mobile Redesign

Blaseball had previously been a desktop-web-only product, and part of the mandate of the 2023 relaunch was to target mobile as a primary platform.

As with the web product, I used the all-new design language and reusable components to create a data-rich experience that could still be played comfortably on the go.
